Shipment Lifecycle Management Report

1. Introduction

This report summarizes shipment distribution, tracking, delivery, and
end-of-journey handling across global operations.

2. Lifecycle Stages

-   Procurement & Planning
-   Packaging & Documentation
-   Transportation & Tracking
-   Customs Clearance
-   Delivery & Receipt
-   Returns & Reverse Logistics

3. Key Metrics

-   Total active shipments
-   On-time delivery rate
-   Average transit time
-   Customs clearance success rate
-   Cost per shipment
-   Return shipment ratio

4. Insights

Most delivery delays occur during customs clearance in emerging markets. Recommendation:
implement automated documentation checks and partner with local customs agents.
