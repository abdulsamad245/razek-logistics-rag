Logistics Procurement Policy

1. Purpose

This policy defines the process for sourcing, approving, and arranging
logistics services for shipments across global locations.

2. Scope

Applies to all company shipments including freight forwarding, warehousing,
transportation, and customs clearance services.

3. Procurement Process

3.1 Service Requests

Departments submit requests through the internal logistics portal, specifying
shipment requirements, destinations, and timelines.

3.2 Approval Workflow

Logistics team validates service requirements. Finance reviews and approves
budget allocations for shipping costs.

3.3 Ordering

Approved logistics services are procured through approved carriers and partners.
All shipments are tracked and registered in the Razek system.

3.4 Delivery

Shipments are handled using Razek's logistics network and tracked until
successful delivery to final destination.

4. Documentation

Invoices, bills of lading, customs declarations, and insurance documents must be uploaded to
Razek's Logistics Document Manager.

5. Risk Management

Lost or damaged shipments must be reported within 24 hours. Claims are filed
with insurance providers. Non-compliant shipments are documented and lessons learned.
