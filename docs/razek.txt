RAZEK - COMPREHENSIVE COMPANY DATA (COMBINED REPORT)

Overview:
Razek is a global logistics platform that enables companies to manage end-to-end supply chain operations, including freight forwarding, warehousing, transportation, and customs clearance. Its services support organizations operating across more than 170 countries, focusing on simplifying international trade and logistics challenges, especially in emerging markets.

Mission:
Razek aims to eliminate barriers to global commerce by delivering seamless logistics solutions and supply chain management. The company focuses on empowering organizations to operate globally without needing to build local logistics infrastructure or handle complex international shipping regulations.

History & Background:
Founded in 2022, Razek operates within the logistics, supply chain management, and international trade industry. Though incorporated in Wilmington, Delaware, Razek operates as a global company, serving clients across multiple continents. The platform was built to solve a major gap in global trade infrastructure—providing reliable logistics management for companies with international supply chains.

Core Services:

1. Freight Forwarding & Transportation
- Helps companies arrange shipping of goods via air, sea, road, and rail from local or global suppliers.
- Provides multimodal transportation solutions with real-time tracking.

2. Global Shipping & Customs Clearance
- Handles international shipments to remote locations across 170+ countries.
- Manages customs documentation, duties, tariffs, and end-to-end delivery tracking.
- Offers cargo insurance and protection options.

3. Warehouse & Inventory Management
- Centralized dashboard for complete inventory tracking across multiple locations.
- Stores shipping manifests, invoices, customs documents, insurance records, and status histories.
- Provides visibility into cargo location, shipment status, and inventory levels.

4. Supply Chain Onboarding/Offboarding
- Handles goods delivery for new suppliers and returns for quality issues.
- Provides secure handling and compliance-focused processing of returned shipments.
- Supports refurbished goods redeployment, storage, or responsible disposal.

5. Compliance & Documentation Management
- Ensures shipments remain compliant with international trade regulations and standards.
- Supports customs declarations, export licenses, and long-term compliance planning.

6. Scalability for Global Operations
- Built for companies with international supply chains spread across multiple countries.
- Especially focused on emerging markets where customs and logistics can be challenging.
- Eliminates the need for internal logistics teams to solve cross-border operations.

Business Model:
Razek uses a mixed subscription + transaction model. Companies can pay per shipment, storage, customs clearance, or logistics action, or subscribe for recurring global trade support. This model supports startups, growing businesses, and large enterprises.

Public Mentions:
Media and logistics outlets describe Razek as a blend of freight marketplace, logistics engine, and supply chain system. It has been highlighted for helping companies streamline international trade and manage global compliance.

Summary:
Razek provides a complete infrastructure for logistics management for globally operating businesses. From procurement to delivery, the platform simplifies the customs and compliance burdens companies face when managing international supply chains.

How Our Story Began
Razek was born from a simple but powerful realization: businesses around the world were struggling to manage their international supply chains due to complex customs regulations, high shipping costs, and fragmented logistics processes—especially in emerging markets. We recognized the need for a better, more efficient way to support global trade and set out to make it easy for companies to expand internationally.
