Supplier Logistics Onboarding Guide

1. Welcome

This guide helps new suppliers integrate with our logistics platform.

2. What You Need to Provide

-   Company registration documents
-   Tax identification
-   Banking information for payments
-   Logistics capability details
-   Insurance certificates

3. Platform Setup

1.  Register on the Razek supplier portal.
2.  Complete KYC verification.
3.  Set up shipping preferences and carrier integrations.

4. Support

For issues, contact the supplier support team through the portal.

5. Responsibilities

Suppliers must maintain accurate inventory data, provide timely shipping updates, and comply
with international trade regulations.
